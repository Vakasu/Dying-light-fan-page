Main page Weapons Enemies Loot

In this game the are lots of different weapons. Melee weapons: knives, machetes, sickles. Range weapons: military rifles, police rifles, pistols, shotguns.

While playing dying light you will meet tons of enemies that would try to kill you at all cost. Biters: viral, goon, gastank, volatile. Humans: Rais, Rais' bandits.

Dying light contains many types of loot that can help you survive in a zombie apocalypse. Power cables, batteries, coffe packages, metal parts, nails...
